About

ELECTRONICS and COMMUNICATION

The department of ECE is one of the major department in Kumaraguru college of technology with the highest number of students. ECE is a fast growing field in engineering. In our college ECE department is located in the C block overlooking the auditorium. Our department was started in the year 1987 with the aim to educate students with knowledge about hardware as well as software. We,as a department have provided every opportunity to enrich and enhance their skills and talents and encourage them to take a step forward in exploring new things. We had students passing out in flying colour with everyone placed in good companies in the past. For the forthcoming years too, we as a team will strive to ensure that each and every student who belong to our department gets his talent recognized and help them pursue their future.
Our department is also known for its department association which is group of students who pledge to work for the department. They conduct various events, seminars, and webinars to provide the students of ECE a chance to enjoy and learn many things. We have a placement team which conducts a series of events called the F40 which is exclusively for the third years to provide training for their placement interviews. Then there is the higher studies team which focuses on students who wish to perceive their higher studies by helping them prepare for entrance examinations. We also have Entrepreneurship team which boosts the students who has business ideas and help them by bringing in speakers and also get contacts with the potential investor’s .Also we have various other teams working behind in ensuring that every student can get to experience new things and learn through their college days apart from their studies. We also have the ECE Times which is magazine which is released by our own student team which highlights the events that took place past month. ECE departments has its own instagram account maintained by the media and marketing team which works everyday posting quizzes ,wishing on birthdays an keeps the account alive. Not just this, we have plenty of initiatives in our department which makes it one of the best departments of our college. Starting from the department association to the teachers, from F40 to ECE times, we stand out with many opportunities in providing the best education and help them achieving their dream.
